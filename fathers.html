Priests Who Shaped Us

This page serves as a tribute and resource to honor all the priests who have faithfully served our parish throughout the years. Through this website, you can search and learn more about the spiritual leaders who have guided our community, celebrated the sacraments among us, and left a lasting impact on our parish life. We are grateful for their service and invite you to explore their stories, photos (if available), and the periods during which they served.

Fathers who Served in the Kattuwa church before becoming a monk

This section honors the devoted priests who faithfully served the Kattuwa Church before answering a deeper call to monastic life. Their spiritual journey began in service to the parish, where they nurtured the faith of the community, celebrated the sacraments, and lived as shepherds among the people. Eventually, they chose the path of consecrated life — embracing prayer, simplicity, and obedience in pursuit of a closer union with God. We remember their contributions with gratitude and continue to pray for their peace and holiness.

The Sons of Preists born from the Kattuwa Sangha

Rev. Fr. Anthony Basil Gamini Silva (Nirmala Maria Sect)

Born on 24 June 1949 in Kattuwa and ordained as a priest on 15 November 1980 at St. Anne's Church, Palathuraya. From 1994 to 2004, he served as the Senior Father of the Congregation of the 'Nazareth' Presbyterian Fathers, Wennappuwa, and from 1980 to 1983, from 1985 to 1991, and from 2012 until his death, he served as a member of the Congregation of the 'Nazareth' Presbyterian Fathers from time to time. Rev. Father Gamini Silva, who served as the Meesam Sevaka at St. Mary's Church, Ampitiya and St. Sebastian's Church, Nochchiyagama, served as a member of the teaching staff of the Ampitiya National Theological Seminary from 2007 to 2012. After a long illness and undergoing treatment, he passed away peacefully on April 5, 2022, leaving behind unforgettable memories.

Rev. Fr. Andrew Shelton Welikadarachi (Colombo Diocese)

Born on 11.05.1955 in Kattuwa, he was ordained a priest on 24 July 1982. He served as the Assistant Father of Mattakkuliya and became the Father of Weliwita Meesam Sevaka. After receiving his education in Rome, he worked as a lecturer at the Ampitiya National Theological Seminary, and after receiving his doctorate in theology in Rome, he worked as the Vice-Rector of the National Theological Seminary and as the Father of St. Joseph's Theological Seminary. He is currently serving as the Colombo Zonal Thero and the Father of Dehiwala Meesam Sevaka. Mr. Rohan Perera is a father.

Rev. Fr. Rohan Perera (Sect of the Divine Svior)

Born on 30.10.1957 in Kattuwa and ordained on 28.11.1985. He first served the Kandy Presbyterian Church. He has served as a parish priest in the parishes of Melsiripura, Kekirawa and Thimbirigasyaya. After receiving higher education in India and the Philippines, he served as a deacon at the Thimbirigasyaya Kanitu Devasathala of the Order of the Divine Savior and the Ampitiya Devasathala of the Order of the Divine Savior. He is currently serving as the parish priest in Hewadivela.

Rev. Fr. Dunstan Piyatissa Welikadarachi (Nirmala Maria Nikaya)

Born on 04.03.1964 in Kattuwa and ordained on 03.08.1991. He first served as a hospital assistant. He served as the parish priest of the parishes of Solewewa, Modara, Wellawaya, and Hambantota in Kurunegala and in the Congregation of the Preachers of the Nazarene in Wennappuwa. Having received higher education in Rome and the Philippines, he is currently serving as the parish priest of Our Lady of the Assumption Church in Maradana.

Rev. Fr. Chanaka Prabath Welikadarachi (Colombo Dioces)

Born on 14.04.1982 in Kattuwa and ordained on 20.04.2013. He was the first priest to be ordained after the Kattuwa was elevated to the rank of Meesama. He served as the assistant priest of Nayakakanda, Kalamulla and Pitipana Meesams. He has been appointed as the Meesam Servant Father of the Ekala New Meesam and is currently serving there.

Rev. Fr. Asiri Devthilina Costa (Colombo Dioces)

Born in Kattuwa on 12.11.1992 and ordained on 06.41.2021. He is currently serving as the Assistant Father of the Parish Priest and is also the YCS Coordinator Father for the East Negombo District.

The Nuns who emerged from the Kattuwa Sangha

Venerable Sister Regina Paris

She was ordained as a nun. Born on 29.12.1913 in Palagathuraya village, Marawila, Sri Lanka, she served as the Mother Superior of the hostel and Wennappuwa monastery on 1935.09.08. She passed away on 27.09.2007. Hon. Milanthi Jayawardena Kana

Venerable Sister Milanthi Jayawardena

Born on 25.41.1978 in Kattuwa, she was ordained as a nun in the Order of the Little Sisters of the Poor on 31.07.2005. She served as a missionary in Australia and France from 2005 to 2022 and is currently serving in India.

Venerable Sister Asangika

After passing the Advanced Level Examination in 2010, she spent 5 years in the Congregation of the Holy Family and became a nun in 2015. During the 6 years from 2015-2021, she was engaged in apostolic work as a pre-school and Dhamma school teacher and in caring for the nuns. She is currently engaged in further training with the Immaculate Conception of Casters Congregation.
