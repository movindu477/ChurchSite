Priests Who Shaped Us

This page serves as a tribute and resource to honor all the priests who have faithfully served our parish throughout the years. Through this website, you can search and learn more about the spiritual leaders who have guided our community, celebrated the sacraments among us, and left a lasting impact on our parish life. We are grateful for their service and invite you to explore their stories, photos (if available), and the periods during which they served.
