Our Church Location

St. Mary’s Church, Kattuwa, stands as a spiritual beacon in the heart of the community. Nestled in the serene surroundings of Kattuwa, our church has been a place of worship, fellowship, and service for generations. Easily accessible and surrounded by peaceful landscapes, it continues to welcome all who seek faith, hope, and love. Discover the sacred space where countless lives have been touched by God’s grace.
