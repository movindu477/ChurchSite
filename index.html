ST. Francis De Sales Church

Nestled in the heart of Kattuwa, Negombo, St. Francis De Sales Church stands as a beacon of faith, hope, and love. Dedicated to our patron, St. Francis de Sales a saint renowned for his deep compassion and gentle spirit our parish warmly welcomes all who seek spiritual growth and community. Whether you're a lifelong Catholic, returning to the Church, or exploring faith for the first time, we invite you to join us in worship, fellowship, and service. Together, as one family in Christ, we strive to live out the Gospel and be a light to our neighbors.
